    What is Tom Atkins' Net Worth?

    Tom Atkins is an American actor who has a net worth of $500 thousand. Tom Atkins was born in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania in November 1935. Atkins is known for his work in horror films and has worked with directors such as Stephen King, John Carpenter, and George A. Romero. He has also frequently portrayed the role of a police officer. He started his career on and off Broadway before moving to Los Angeles, California. His debut television role came in 1963 in the series The Doctors.

    In 1974 he had a recurring role as Sgt. Frank Cole in the TV series Harry O. Atkins starred as Lt. Tom Sullivan from 1976 to 1977 and had a recurring role in the series The Rockford Files from 1974 to 1977. Atkins has starred in many films including The Detective, The Fog, The Ninth Configuration, Escape from New York, Creepshow, Halloween III: Season of the Witch, Night of the Creeps, Lethal Weapon, Maniac Cop, Striking Distance, Bruiser, My Bloody Valentine, and Drive Angry. In 2010 Atkins was inducted into the Fangoria Horror Hall of Fame and won the Horror Icon of the Year Award at the Louisville Fight Night Film Fest.
