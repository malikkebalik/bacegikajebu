    Leroy Kemp is an American freestyle wrestler who was born on 24 December, 1956 in Cleveland, Ohio, United States. He is currently 67 years old. Kemp began his wrestling career in high school, where he was a three-time state champion. He went on to compete in college, where he was a two-time NCAA Division I All-American. He also competed in the 1984 Summer Olympics, where he placed fifth in the freestyle wrestling competition. Kemp has also had a successful career as a coach, having coached at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, the University of Minnesota, and the University of Iowa.     While competing for the University of Wisconsin–Madison Lee Kemp was a four-time NCAA Division I National finalist, winning the championship three times and placing 2nd, on a split referee's decision, as a true freshman at 18 years old.
