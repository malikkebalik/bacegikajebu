    Whos Behind the Squirrel Mask on the Masked Singer?

    Every season of Masked Singer features a new group of famous faces hidden behind intricate masks and every season of Masked Singer fans guess who they are by tweeting their best predictions. And were here to help you identify the celebrities hiding behind these zanier-than-ever masks.

    18 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Every season of Masked Singer features a new group of famous faces hidden behind intricate masks and every season of Masked Singer fans guess who they are by tweeting their best predictions. And we’re here to help you identify the celebrities hiding behind these zanier-than-ever masks.

    Fans of the show The Masked Singer are still attempting to guess who is hiding under the Squirrel mask. On March 8, 2023, voters approved The Squirrel as the tournament champion.

    Who is Squirrel on Masked Singer?

    The Squirrel-Costumed Masked Singer sang P!nk’s “Try” as they took the stage. The Masked singer shared a Tweet on their official Twitter account which showed the epic performance of Squirrel Mask.

    KaPOW! 💥 #SquirrelMask gave an epic performance last night. 🎉 #TheMaskedSinger pic.twitter.com/PJxKIriaPI

    — The Masked Singer (@MaskedSingerFOX) March 9, 2023

    The show’s judges reasoned that it may be one of the following people behind the squirrel mask:

    • Robin Thicke guessed Uma Thurman
    • Ken Jeong guessed Michelle Moynahan, Renee Zellweger and Margot Robbie
    • Jenny McCarthy guessed Naomi Watts or Katie Holmes

    Others on Twitter also made assumptions, such as:

    • Kaley Cuoco
    • Cameron Diaz
    • Demi Moore
    • Scarlett Johansson
    • Malin Akerman

    What Were the Clues of Squirrel?

    The Squirrel, like the other singers has their own special clues to help them win the competition. These included:

    • They were a professional figure skater
    • A smiley face button
    • “Empathy” was their superpower
    • A plate of Swedish meatballs
    • A gorilla
    • She worked for a major modeling agency
    • She did teen acne commercials
    • She was in several international sitcoms
    • She has made out with Tom Cruise
    • A castle

    The Masked singer shared a Tweet on their verified account and said “We’re never taking #SquirrelMask for granted”!

    We know one thing for sure — we’re never taking #SquirrelMask for granted! 🤩👏 #TheMaskedSinger pic.twitter.com/DHv2tO2R0l

    — The Masked Singer (@MaskedSingerFOX) March 12, 2023
