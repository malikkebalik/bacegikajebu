    Katherine Cunningham is an American actress. She began her Hollywood career in the 2010s where she debuted in an episode of Detroit 1-8-7. Her first recurring role was as Bunny Kate in the show The Playboy Club. Afterward, she played the role of Mindi in the show I Made America. She also made her film debut in 2012’s Two Days in February. She next played recurring character Corinne in Chicago Fire and starred in the miniseries Underemployed. In 2014, she had a role in the movie Bad Johnson, after which she played Beth Scott in the TV series Mind Games. This year, she gained recognition for starring as Kathy Hale in Condor and playing Christina in Yellowstone.
